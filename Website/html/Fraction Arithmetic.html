Function : Find Roots

Description : Finds the roots of the quadratic equation.

Background : Roots are the values where the quadratic equation becomes zero or touches the x axis.


To factor a quadratic eqaution of the form ax^2+bx+c=0
Enter values of a,b and c.
Example :
If the Equation is 5x^2-13x-14=0
Then a = 5, b = -13 , c = -14.
